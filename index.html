Instructions
1. Prepare your CSV Your file must include these required headers (additional columns are allowed):
AttendeeName, Street, City, PostalCode, E-mail, AttendeePhone, DateOfBirth
2. Upload & Select Upload your CSV file, select the correct Lifesaving Society form from the dropdown, then select the matching template PDF from the templates_pdf folder.
3. Generate & Download Click "Generate PDFs". A ZIP file containing all filled test sheets will download automatically.
Privacy Notice This tool runs entirely in your browser. No data is uploaded or sent anywhere. All processing happens locally on your machine.
